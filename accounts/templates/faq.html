Frequently Asked Questions

How do you use Priced Out?

First, get started with Priced Out by signing up on its homepage or simply click on a product card on the homepage to immediately compare prices of that product between Amazon and E-bay. Signing up gives you the unique advantage of sharing your favorite products with family and friends on social media to comment on.

When will the featured products update/change on this site?

The Priced Out website will update/change its featured products once every month.

Does the price comparison website give accurate rates based on Amazon and eBay listed pricing?

Yes, the Priced Out website will display accurate rates, and its listed products are consistently price checked.

Will pricing automatically update if the Amazon or ebay seller makes a product price change?

Priced Out works diligently to update its website within 1 hour of product price changes, including price changes made by Amazon and E-bay sellers.
